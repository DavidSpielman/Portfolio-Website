Reverse Engineering: Nail Dryer Fan

  • date: September 2019 - December 2019

The objective of this project was to recreate a low-cost battery operated device or toy using only SolidWorks features. The device our group chose was a MelodySuzie portable nail dryer fan that is used to reduce the time needed to dry nails after applying nail polish. Creating a SolidWorks assembly of the nail dryer fan would have greatly simplified the modelling process, but creating the fan through SolidWorks features gave my group and me the opportunity to explore and become familiar with a wide array of SolidWorks features. This project was made as a final project for ME 14500 at CCNY. I worked in a team of four to discuss how to best model our chosen device, however each group member created their own individual SolidWorks model of the fan. The images of the SolidWorks model below were made by me.

Prior to creating the SolidWorks model, we observed the outside and inside components of the fan and tested the product.

Dimensions of the inside and outside design features of the fan were measured, and a technical sketch of the part was created.

Once this step was complete, the single feature SolidWorks model was created. Some aspects of the fan design had to be simplified such as the fan blades, wire connections, and the raised shell of the fan exterior. Images of the outside of the final model can be seen below.

Transparent views of the nail dryer fan model can be seen below.

After studying the product for many hours while creating the SolidWorks model, our team came up with a few suggestions to improve the product. The fan is battery operated, but its design also included a DC power jack. To improve the portability of the product and reduce the cost of manufacturing the product by $0.60, we recommend removing this component. Additionally, the product width should be made wider to allow users to place their entire hand inside the device more comfortably. Also, it could be beneficial to include a mechanism to produce warm or cool air based on the user's preferences.
